Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station located in Himachal Pradesh, India. It is famous for its snow-capped mountains, scenic valleys, and pleasant weather, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley – Famous for adventure sports like paragliding and skiing.
  2. Rohtang Pass – Known for its snowy landscapes and breathtaking views.
  3. Hadimba Temple – A peaceful temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  4. Old Manali – Popular for its cafes and relaxed vibe.
  5. Vashisht Hot Springs – Natural hot water springs with healing properties.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – A traditional Himachali steamed bread.
  • Thukpa – A warm and comforting noodle soup.
  • Chana Madra – A rich yogurt-based curry.
  • Momos – Popular steamed dumplings loved by tourists.
  • Babru – A local stuffed bread similar to kachori.
