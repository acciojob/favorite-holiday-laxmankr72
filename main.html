Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station known for its snow-covered mountains and pleasant climate.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley – Famous for adventure sports.
  2. Rohtang Pass – Known for snow views.
  3. Hadimba Temple – Surrounded by forests.
  4. Old Manali – Popular for cafes.
  5. Vashisht Hot Springs – Natural hot water baths.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – Traditional Himachali food.
  • Thukpa – Hot noodle soup.
  • Chana Madra – Creamy curd-based curry.
  • Momos – Famous dumplings.
  • Babru – Local stuffed bread.
